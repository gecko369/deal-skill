## Celebration FL entity dictionary

### Official identifiers
- **Full name:** Celebration, Florida
- **ZIP code:** 34747
- **County:** Osceola County
- **State:** Florida
- **Incorporated:** Unincorporated community (not a city — governed by Osceola County)
- **Founded:** 1994 by The Walt Disney Company; residents began moving in 1996
- **Geographic note:** Located 6 miles southwest of Walt Disney World, 20 miles from Orlando International Airport

### Key governance and institutions
- **CROA:** Celebration Residential Owners Association — master HOA governing all properties in Celebration. Every buyer pays a one-time contribution at closing (typically 1% of purchase price) plus monthly fees. Fees range roughly $280-$490/month depending on neighborhood and home type.
- **Celebration Community Foundation:** nonprofit running many community events and programs
- **Osceola County School District:** governs Celebration's public schools

### Schools
- **Celebration K-8 School** — public (Osceola County School District); highly rated
- **Celebration High School** — public (Osceola County); rated among better high schools in Osceola County
- **Bridgewater Academy** (formerly Celebration School) — the original K-12 that transitioned
- Private options nearby: Faith Christian Academy, Harmony Academy, various charter schools in the district

### Neighborhoods and villages
- **North Village** — oldest neighborhood; historic Celebration-style architecture; lower HOA
- **South Village** — diverse architecture; near town center
- **West Village** — larger lots; quieter; single-family homes
- **Spring Valley** — family-oriented; near the nature reserve
- **Artisan Park** — newer (2006+); townhomes and SFR; sub-HOA plus CROA
- **Aquila Reserve** — gated; luxury tier
- **Mirasol** — newer townhome community at the south end
- **Water Tower Place** — mid-range; near Celebration Ave commercial corridor

### Landmarks and places
- **Celebration Town Center** — the walkable downtown core; restaurants, shops, boutiques along Front Street and Market Street
- **Lakeside Park** — main community park; site of the Celebration Farmers Market (Sundays) and many community events
- **Lake Rianhard** — the central lake in the town center; surrounded by boardwalk and dining
- **Celebration Golf Club** — semi-private 18-hole course
- **Celebration Nature Reserve** — over 4,700 acres of protected wetlands adjacent to the community
- **Celebration Place** — commercial district along Celebration Ave; offices and hotels
- **Celebration Health / Florida Hospital Celebration Health** — AdventHealth campus within the community

### Key streets and corridors
- **Front Street** — main restaurant/retail strip along Lake Rianhard
- **Market Street** — primary town center pedestrian corridor
- **Celebration Avenue** — main auto-access spine through the community
- **US-192 (W Irlo Bronson Memorial Hwy)** — southern commercial boundary
- **I-4** — 5 miles north; primary highway connection

### Recurring events (ongoing series — always eligible for event posts)
- **Celebration Farmers Market** — Sundays, Lakeside Park, year-round
- **Celebration Movie in the Park** — seasonal; Lakeside Park
- **Celebration of Lights** — November/December; town center holiday event
- **Independence Day celebration at Celebration** — July 4th; fireworks at Lakeside Park
- **Easter in the Park** — spring
- **Halloween in Celebration** — October; town center trick-or-treat
- **Celebration Marathon / 5K events** — varies by year
- **Downtown dining events** — various seasonal promotions by town center merchants
